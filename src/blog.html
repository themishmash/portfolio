Losing Toenails on the Rwenzori Mountains in Western Uganda

10 July 2018 by Michelle Wong

The mountains of the moon. Sounds pretty alluring doesn’t it? This is the name given to the Rwenzori mountains of Uganda, named after their snow capped peaks that resemble the moon. It’s also the reason I lost not one, but two toenails whilst summiting one of its peaks. I don’t regret it. Not one bit. In 2016 I first encountered these mountains whilst reading the book Ubuntu, where the author Heather Ellis travelled by motorcycle, solo, across most of Africa. Spoiler alert: It was an entertaining read but I thought to myself, she is crazy. Like completely and utterly batty.
